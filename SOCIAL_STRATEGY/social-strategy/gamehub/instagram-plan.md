## Visual Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Electric Blue (#00D4FF) - links, CTAs
- **Secondary:** Neon Pink (#FF00FF) - accents, highlights
- **Background:** Deep Black (#0A0A0A) - posts, stories
- **Accent:** Arcade Yellow (#FFD700) - scores, badges

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Retro pixel font
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar)
- **Emphasis:** All-caps for game names

### Post Templates
- **Grid style:** 3-column aesthetic (maintain theme)
- **Story style:** Bold text + gameplay clip
- **Reel style:** Fast cuts + trending audio

---

## Post Types & Specs

### Carousel Posts (Most Engaging)
- **Format:** 4-10 slides
- **Ideas:**
  - "5 games for when you're bored"
  - "Beginner ‚Üí Expert progression"
  - "Game evolution: 1980 vs 2024"

### Reels (Growth Engine)
- **Length:** 15-30 seconds
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds MUST grab attention
- **Audio:** Trending sounds or arcade SFX
- **Text:** Big, readable captions

### Stories (Community Building)
- **Polls:** "Which game should we add?"
- **Quizzes:** "Guess the game from screenshot"
- **Countdowns:** New game launches
- **Q&A:** AMA about games

### Static Posts (Evergreen)
- **Game screenshots with overlaid text**
- **Quote cards:** Gaming wisdom
- **Infographics:** "Types of arcade games"
