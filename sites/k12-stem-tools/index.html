Our Philosophy

AI as Calculator 2.0

Just as we didn't ban calculators, we shouldn't fear AI. The question isn't "should students use AI?" but "what skills become MORE valuable when AI handles the routine?"

Progressive Independence

Younger students need structured guidance. Older students should learn to collaborate with AI as a tool‚Äîknowing when to use it, when to question it, and when to work without it.

Human Skills Amplified

Critical thinking, creativity, ethical reasoning, and human connection become MORE important, not less. AI handles the syntax; humans provide the judgment.
